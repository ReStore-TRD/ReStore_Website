Projects!

RESTORE PROJECT

ReStore receives donations from small kitchen items, to big sofas and shelves! everything except mattresses! We take in the donated items and collaborate with the drivers, for items received during pick-up shifts. The donations are sorted to different categories and stored in the storages and taken into inventory. Then we give away the items, usually there are opening days in the beginning of the semesters! One of the main tasks of this project group is to plan this big day at ReStore! An event full of decision making, group work and meeting new people!

RESYKKEL

Students bring their clean, ready-to-ride bikes during designated drop-off times. Sit has also agreed to donate bicycles abandoned on their property. Student signs agreement telling us where to send the money when their bike sells, and what to do with the bike in case it doesn't sell. We take a fixed fee to run ReStore. We sell bikes on behalf of the departed students We will also repair abandoned bicycles and sell these, returning them to the market.

REBUILD

The group where we give new life to trash Broken donations get sorted in the ReBuild workshop During weekly workshops and using tools (sewing machine, power tools and others) we fix furniture Sometimes we make new furnitures from scratch using the donated material or redesign furniture donated After every meet, we drink hot chocolate and eat snacks. Sometimes we cook and eat dinner together. We also organize specific workshops around sewing (making christmas reusable wrapping paper for instance). If you’re want to do some crafting and learn how to fix things but also to meet nice people and spend time with them, join us next Monday !

REHOME

Cleaning and rearranging our two buildings to make volunteering spaces pleasant! Made with furniture from donations! Including a football table, tablet table and other interesting items. Volunteers use these spaces for socializing, meetings, workshops, working on projects and more. But always *room* for improvement!

DRIVERS

Large furniture is more difficult to move, we don’t want this to be of hinderance for reuse! During the semester openings, we help out transporting the bigger items for a modest fee. Supporting the other projects in transportation needs.

SOCIAL MEDIA

Maintaining the facebook and instagram pages, creating events, answering queries on FB/messenger/IG. Making illustrations, posters, taking photos, posting stories, feed, polls, etc. Creating a bridge between people and the restore team, board, in between different projects. Trying to reach more people, broadening exposure to social platforms.
