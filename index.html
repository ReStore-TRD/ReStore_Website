Who are we?

Restore is a voluntary student organisation that works to reduce waste from household equipment and furniture and improve quality of student life by facilitating reuse of goods for all students at NTNU.

From student to student

Moving on- If you’ve got items you don’t need anymore but are too good to throw away, bring them to ReStore!

Moving in- check our opening dates on our social media channels (instagram/facebook) and come pick up furniture you need- for free!

New life for used goods

Making new products consumes high amounts of energy, resources and causes environmental polution. By reusing of furniture, kitchen utensils and many other household items, ReStore aims to extend the life cycle of products.
